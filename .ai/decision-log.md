# Decision Log

Stable decisions live here after review. New entries are append-only unless correcting a factual error.

## What Belongs Here vs. in Nap Checkpoints

- **Decision log** captures *what was chosen and why*: the decision, the alternatives that were rejected, the governor outcome, and any follow-up commitments. One entry per load-bearing decision.
- **Nap checkpoints** (`nap-checkpoints.md`) capture *where the system is*: what changed in this merge, what is now proven, what remains uncertain, and what the next reasoning cycle should answer. One entry per merge.

A single PR can produce zero, one, or several decision-log entries (usually one), and exactly one nap checkpoint.
